Welcome to my website on Orcas!

Orcas are a beauty. They are vicious (reason why they are named "Wolves of the Sea") but that's why I like them: they protect their family and are loving with their close ones.

Fun Facts about Orcas

  • They can live up to 90 years in the wild! Wooaahh!
  • They are the largest members of the oceanic dolphin family
  • Orcas have no natural predators and are at the top of the food chain.
  • Orcas are one of the only non-human animals known to have evolved based on culture, with different populations having unique hunting methods and social behaviors.
